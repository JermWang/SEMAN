Why are we doing this?

Honestly? It started as a joke. We spelled a legend's name backwards and it looked kind of like "semen", so naturally he became a little guy who swims. Classic.

But here's the thing — an entire generation is going to grow up in a world of wallets, tokens, and scams they don't understand. So we decided our dumb little coin should actually do something good: fund real crypto and financial-literacy education for kids.

Buy the coin, laugh at the meme, and a portion swims straight toward teaching the next generation how not to get rugged. That's it. That's the mission.
